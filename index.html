About Me

Hi! My name is Alejandra Lara. I'm Mexican-American and currently in my third year at Syracuse University studying Info Mgmt. & Tech with a concentration in Information Security Mgmt.

Alongside being a student, I am currently a Software Engineering Fellow at HeadStarter AI. Additionally, I am also a student at CodePath, partaking in their Internship Connection Program.

I have experience in Python, Haskell, HTML/CSS and I am in the process of learning JS. I believe myself to be motivated and dedicated, allowing for my work ethic to be high standard. I put my best foot forward in everything I attempt. While my technical skills may not be fully developed yet, I am always willing to learn and be the best that I can be.

Beyond the corporate world, I am an avid reader, art lover, and video game enthusiast. I spend my free time looking for new novels to read or playing MOBA games. I absolutely adore Art and spend the occasional weekend at Art galleries.
